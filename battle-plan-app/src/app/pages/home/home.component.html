Battle Plan

With this app, you can easily manage and track the turn order for any tabletop role playing game.

  • Turn Initiative scores on or off to suit any type of game. While on, combatants are sorted in descending order. While off, combatants are simply added to the list in the order they are entered.
  • Add Players, Monsters, and NPCs before the battle begins, then dynamically add and remove combatants during combat.
  • Click on any combatant's Name, Type, or Score to edit and automatically update the list.
  • Use the Save feature to store a party's information, then the Load feature to quickly add them to each new battle.
  • Clear the entire list to get ready for the next showdown.
