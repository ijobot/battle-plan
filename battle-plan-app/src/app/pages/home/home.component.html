Battle Plan

Now you can track your combat turn order for any tabletop role playing game!

Add players, monsters, and NPCs to the battlefield or remove them just as easily.

Combatants are automatically sorted by their initiative score. You can dynamically add or remove combatants mid-fight, edit names or scores, or clear the entire list to get ready for the next battle. You can even save your party information to easily submit multiple combatants all at once!
